why choose us

AKUS Pvt. Ltd. offers a compelling choice for individuals and businesses seeking reliable product authentication services. Here are a few reasons to choose us:
a) Comprehensive Verification: Our AKUS Generic Authenticator tool is designed to verify products across diverse categories, regardless of brand or purchase location. We ensure that users have access to a robust and all-encompassing authentication platform.
b) Trusted and Reliable: With our tool, users can confidently determine the authenticity of their products, providing peace of mind and protecting them from counterfeit or fraudulent items. Our commitment to trust and reliability sets us apart.
c) Industry Collaboration: We sync our platform with multiple vendors' databases, establishing a collaborative network that enhances the accuracy and effectiveness of our product authentication process. This integration allows us to deliver an extensive and reliable service.
d) Free for Users: We believe in providing our product authentication service free of charge to users, prioritizing their satisfaction and enabling them to make informed purchasing decisions without any additional costs.

what we do

At AKUS Pvt. Ltd., we have developed the AKUS Generic Authenticator tool, which serves as a comprehensive solution for product authentication. Here's an overview of what we do:
a) Multi-Vendor Database Integration: We synchronize our platform with databases from various vendors, enabling us to cross-reference and verify products against the criteria established by different companies.
b) Cross-Category Verification: Our tool is capable of authenticating products from a wide range of categories, including but not limited to packaged food items, mechanical hardware, computer and IT products, healthcare supplies, automotive parts, and more.
c) Subscription-Based Model: We offer vendors the opportunity to subscribe to our services, granting them access to our extensive database and verification capabilities. This subscription-based model allows vendors to enhance customer trust and confidence in their products.

AKUS

Our company has developed a tool called AKUS Generic Authenticator, designed to assist users in verifying the authenticity of their products. This tool seamlessly integrates with multiple vendors' databases to check users' products against the specific criteria established by various companies. It offers a comprehensive solution that transcends the factors of purchase location, brand, or product type. Whether it is a packaged food item or a mechanical hardware product, our platform can verify products across all categories. We take pride in offering this service free of charge to all users, aiming to ensure their peace of mind regarding the legitimacy of their purchases. However, we do operate on a subscription-based model for vendors who wish to avail themselves of our authentication services. By subscribing to our platform, vendors can leverage our comprehensive database and verification capabilities to enhance their customers' trust and confidence in their products. Our authentication process is designed to provide you with the assurance that the products you possess are genuine. We understand the importance of trust and reliability when it comes to consumer goods, and our tool aims to deliver on those fronts. Thank you for considering our services.
